# Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
Analyzed 9,000+ sales records from the Kaggle Superstore dataset to uncover revenue trends, customer behaviour, and regional performance across the US market.
Performed end-to-end data cleaning & transformation in SQL (MySQL) and built an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize key business insights.

- SQL (MySQL)
- Power BI

## Dashboard Features
- Total Sales and KPI overview  
- Sales trend over time  
- Sales by category and segment  
- Top customers by sales  
- **Map visual** — geographic sales distribution across US states
- **Date slicer** — filter all visuals by custom date range

---

## 
- Total sales reached **2.29M**, indicating strong overall performance.  
- Sales are heavily concentrated in key regions such as California and New York.  
- The **Consumer segment** dominates sales, contributing more than half of total revenue, making it the primary customer base.  
- The **Technology category** generates the highest sales, indicating strong demand for tech products.  
- Sales show an **overall upward trend over time**, with periodic spikes suggesting seasonal or promotional effects.  
- A small number of customers contribute significantly to total revenue.  
- A few products dominate overall sales, indicating **product concentration**. 

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing regions to maximize revenue growth.  
- Strengthen marketing efforts for the Consumer segment.  
- Invest more in Technology category as it drives the highest sales.  
- Target high-value customers with loyalty programs.  
- Promote top-performing products to increase sales further.
